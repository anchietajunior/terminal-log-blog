## Jose Anchieta

**Software Engineer** · **University Lecturer** · **AI Graduate Student**

---

I've been writing code for years. I teach others to do the same. Now I study how machines can learn on their own.

This blog is where these three worlds meet: the engineering of building systems, the craft of explaining them, and the research of pushing them forward.

---

### What you'll find here

- **Software Engineering** — architectures, tools, patterns, and the decisions that make the difference
- **Teaching & Didactics** — reflections on explaining complex concepts and training developers
- **Artificial Intelligence** — graduate research notes, paper reviews, experiments with ML and LLMs
- **Computing & Culture** — on the history of machines and the people who built them
